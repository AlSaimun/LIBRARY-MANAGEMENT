Library Rules

1 Membership:

Membership is required to borrow books from the library. You can sign up for free and become a member.

2 Borrowing :

You can borrow book there are no limit. but you cant borrow same book and we borrow a book for 15 days.

3 Return Period:

The standard return period for borrowed books is 15 days. After that, a fine will be charged for each overdue day.

4 Fine:

A fine will be charged for late book returns. The fine amount is Five TAKA per day.

6 Damaged Books:

If a book is returned in damaged condition, the member will be charged for its repair or replacement.

7 Lost Books:

If a book is lost, the member will be responsible for the cost of the book.
